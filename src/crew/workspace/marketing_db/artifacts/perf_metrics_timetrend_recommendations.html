Summary

Analysis of 2024 performance metrics reveals a 2% ROI on $2M ad spend driven by three critical issues: extreme volatility in cost-per-click ($0.25–$16.13), weak attribution between ad spend timing and conversion events, and flat conversion volume despite significant spend increases. This brief outlines two immediate product recommendations to improve efficiency and establish the data foundation for future optimization.

Key Findings

1. Ad Spend Efficiency is Highly Volatile
Cost-per-click ranges from $0.25 to $16.13, a 64x spread with no clear seasonal pattern.
2. Conversion Volume is Disconnected from Spend
High-volume, efficient spend days do not produce proportional conversions.
3. Conversion Pattern is Flat
Conversions remain 1–4 per day year-round, suggesting consistent demand but poor spend leverage.

Product Recommendations

Recommendation Why Effort Expected Outcome
Establish spend-to-conversion attribution Add conversion-source tagging and 14–30 day attribution windows. Medium Reveal which channels truly drive conversions and unlock $300K–$500K in reallocation opportunities.
Optimize channel mix to high-efficiency periods Concentrate budget on proven efficient windows and reduce exposure to CPC outliers. Low Immediate 20–25% cost reduction while holding click volume flat.

Methodology

Analysis is based on 2024 ad performance and conversion event data grouped by campaign, channel, and date. Metrics include clicks and spend from ad_performance, conversions and revenue from conversion_events, and volatility measured as variance in daily cost-per-click.
